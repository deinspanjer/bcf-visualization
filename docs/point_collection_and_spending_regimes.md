# Point collection and spending regime notes

## Baseline rule (chapter 1)

- 2000 words written = 100 CP earned.
- Roll attempted every 100 CP earned.
- A perk is purchased when banked CP meets the rolled cost; otherwise the roll misses and CP remains banked.

## Chapter 91 change ("The Toolkit")

- Roll cadence changed from **100 CP/roll** to **200 CP/roll**.
- Earning rate remained **2000 words / 100 CP**.
- Effective cadence changed from **2000 words/roll** to **4000 words/roll**.

## Chapter 97 changes ("Confrontations")

1. **Base earning slowed** from 2000 words/100 CP to **3000 words/100 CP**.
2. **Recovery shadow on 600/800 perks**:
   - 600-cost acquisition: 300-CP shadow (9000 words at zero CP gain).
   - 800-cost acquisition: 400-CP shadow (12000 words at zero CP gain).
