# Chart pack guide

Quick reference for static chart outputs in `figures/`.

- `publish_pace.png` — cumulative words vs publish date with rule-change context.
- `throughput_by_year.png` — yearly pace small multiples.
- `throughput_by_regime.png` — throughput comparison by mechanic regime.
- `words_per_perk.png` — words between paid acquisitions over time.
- `monthly_throughput.png` — monthly chapters, words, and median chapter size.
- `rolls_per_chapter.png` — curator-range hit/miss distribution.
- `acquisitions_per_chapter.png` — paid vs free acquisitions by chapter.
- `constellation_growth.png` — per-constellation cumulative growth.
- `time_dilation.png` — in-world day progression vs real-world time and words.
